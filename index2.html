About Yoneyama Mai

Overview

Graduated from the Tokyo Institute of Design. Joined GAINAX in 2009 and worked there for five years. In 2014, she left GAINAX to join TRIGGER as a freelancer, following Masahiko Otsuka, Yoyuki Imaishi, and Kazuya Masumoto. In 2016, began to work on illustration. From December 6, 2019 to December 25, 2019, SHE will be held at pixiv WAEN GALLERY. In 2020, she joined SSS by applibot, a creative studio owned by the game company applibot. Personal exhibition "EGO" will be held from April 3 to April 14, 2021.
